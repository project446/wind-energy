The Modern Wind Wheel

From ancient Persian windmills to 15MW offshore giants.

Kinetic Capture

Modern blades use aerodynamic lift (like an airplane wing) rather than simple pressure. This allows them to spin faster than the wind itself.

The Nacelle

Inside the hub, a gearbox increases rotation speeds from 15 RPM to 1,800 RPM to feed the copper-wired generator.
