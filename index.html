What is Wind Energy?

Wind energy is electricity made from the power of wind.

We use very tall machines called wind turbines to catch the wind and turn it into electricity.

How Does a Wind Turbine Work?

  1. Wind blows ‚Üí spins the blades
  2. Blades turn a rotor (like a big fan)
  3. Rotor spins a generator inside
  4. Generator makes electricity
  5. Electricity travels through wires to our homes
