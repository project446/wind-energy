Wind Energy to Electrical Energy

Learn how wind becomes electricity – Class 7 Science Project

What is Wind Energy?

Wind is moving air.
Fast moving air = lots of kinetic energy

How do we change wind energy into electricity?

Step 1
Wind pushes the blades of the windmill (rotor).
Step 2
Blades start rotating → shaft also rotates.
Step 3
Shaft is connected to a generator inside the nacelle.
Step 4
Generator changes mechanical energy → electrical energy.
Step 5
Electricity travels through wires → transformer → our homes!

Wind Turbine – Main Parts

Main parts: Blades • Nacelle (generator inside) • Tower • Foundation

Did you know?
→ A single large wind turbine can give electricity to 300–600 houses!
→ Wind energy is clean energy → no smoke, no pollution
→ India is among the top 5 countries in wind power production

Advantages of Wind Energy

  • Does not pollute air or water
  • Never finishes (renewable)
  • After making turbine – electricity is almost free
  • Good for villages far from cities

Disadvantages (Problems)

  • Works only when wind blows fast
  • Needs large open space
  • Birds sometimes get hurt
  • Makes some noise
